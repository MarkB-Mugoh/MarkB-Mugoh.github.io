Project Description

Market X is a tool for young people pursuing entrepreneurship endeavors , supplemental education, and an environment that supports their personal and professional development. Market X exclusively supports local youth who need access to the local market, business support, training, and assistance building business capacity. Market X will connect community members who desire to support local businesses, post bids for jobs/services, and are interested in stimulating the local economy.
